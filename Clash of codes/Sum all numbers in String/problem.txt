Goal
Sum all the numbers in the string.

Input
Text Line of text with numbers.

Output
num Sum of the numbers in the text.

Constraints
- All the numbers are always integers.
- Symbol "-" before a number makes the number negative.

Example

Input
"I have 2 apples and 3 grapes"

Output
5
